# Privacy Principles

PocketPass and future B3YOND products should follow these privacy principles:

1. Share only what the user chooses to share.
2. Never broadcast sensitive personal data over Bluetooth.
3. Use temporary identifiers for nearby discovery.
4. Make privacy settings easy to understand.
5. Avoid collecting unnecessary location data.
6. Do not sell personal data.
7. Build privacy into the product from the beginning.
