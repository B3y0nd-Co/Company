# Company Roadmap

## Short-Term: 0–12 Months

- Build Android MVP for PocketPass.
- Validate the concept with real users.
- Conduct competitive and patent research.
- Launch a private beta.
- Collect feedback and iterate.

## Mid-Term: 1–3 Years

- Expand to iOS.
- Partner with event organizers.
- Reach product-market fit.
- Build sustainable recurring revenue.

## Long-Term: 3–10 Years

Establish B3YOND Co. as a respected technology company with multiple products that improve how people connect and interact in the real world.
