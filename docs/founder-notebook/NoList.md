# What We Will Never Become

B3YOND Co. will not become a company that sacrifices trust for growth.

## We Will Never

- Sell users' personal data.
- Prioritize growth over privacy.
- Add features simply because competitors have them.
- Sacrifice product quality for speed.
- Compromise user trust for short-term gains.
- Build products that manipulate users into staying longer without purpose.
- Hide important privacy choices behind confusing settings.
