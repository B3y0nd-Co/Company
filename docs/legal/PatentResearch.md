# Patent Research

## Purpose

Track prior art and possible patentable technical ideas.

## Important Note

B3YOND Co. should not assume the broad idea of proximity-based networking is patentable.

Possible protectable areas may include specific technical implementations, such as:

- Privacy-preserving encounter verification
- Repeated encounter confidence scoring
- Context-specific identity cards
- Mutual unlock systems
- Anti-spoofing methods
- Event-based proximity verification

## Research Backlog

- Bluetooth Low Energy architecture
- Background discovery limitations on Android and iOS
- Privacy-preserving identity exchange
- Proximity networking patents
- Event networking platforms
- Dating apps using crossed-path logic
